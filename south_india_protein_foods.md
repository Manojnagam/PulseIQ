## 📋 Quick Reference for Wellness Clients

### For Weight Loss (high protein, low fat):

### For Muscle Gain (high protein + calories):
- Whole eggs, chicken thighs, peanut butter, soy chunks, paneer, mutton

### For Vegetarians:
- Soy chunks, paneer, groundnuts, urad dal, pesarattu, sattu, curd

### For Budget-conscious clients:
- Eggs, sardines, groundnuts, soy chunks, toor dal, sattu
