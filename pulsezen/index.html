FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge to get started? ▼
No. You just message us on WhatsApp with your center details. We build the page, set everything up and send you your link. You don't need to touch any code.
What is area exclusivity? ▼
Only one wellness center per locality gets a PulseZen page. If you are in Old Town Nandyal, no other center in Old Town gets PulseZen. Your area is locked for you as long as you have an active subscription.
What if my area is already taken? ▼
You go on the waitlist for free. If the center in your area cancels their subscription, you get first priority. You can also take a non-exclusive listing at ₹299/month — you get a page but without the area lock.
How long does it take to get my page live? ▼
Within 24 hours of registering. We ask for your center name, location, WhatsApp number, timings and any photos you have. We build the page and send you the link the same day or next morning.
Can I update my page later? ▼
Yes. Any time you want to add new results, update timings, change photos or add testimonials — just message us on WhatsApp. Updates are done within 24 hours.
Is there any contract or lock-in period? ▼
No contract. Month to month. You can cancel anytime. But remember — if you cancel, your area exclusivity is released and another center can claim it.
What is PulseIQ and is it different from PulseZen? ▼
PulseZen is your public website — what new customers see. PulseIQ is your internal management dashboard — attendance, customer data, finance, body scans. They work together but are separate tools. PulseIQ is included in the Elite plan.
