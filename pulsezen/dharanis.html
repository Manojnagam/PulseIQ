Our Story

Why Dharani's Wellness Center Exists

This center was born from a deeply personal journey — a mother's 18-year struggle, and the transformation that changed everything.

Before → After
Founder's Journey

From 18 Years of Struggle
to Helping Hundreds Heal

For nearly two decades, our founder Lakshmidevi Nagam lived with multiple health challenges simultaneously:

⚡ Fits & Seizures — 18 years 🦴 Back Pain — 12 years ⚖️ Was 77 kg → Now 63 kg 💧 Low Blood Pressure 🫁 Sinus Headaches 🦵 Sciatica & Knee Pain 🦶 Foot Pain 😴 Poor Sleep 🌀 Gas Trouble 🚿 Digestion Problems 💪 Body Pains

"I had been struggling for 18 years — raising 3 children while managing so many health issues at once. Doctors helped, but the daily discomfort never fully went away. I had accepted it as my life."

In 2020, everything changed. Lakshmidevi discovered a structured nutrition and wellness program and began her journey with complete consistency. Over the following months, her energy returned, her weight dropped from 77 kg to 63 kg, her sleep improved, and most of her long-standing health challenges reduced significantly — including conditions she had lived with for nearly two decades.

The transformation gave her a purpose.
She realized that so many people in Nandyal were quietly suffering the same way — not knowing that the right nutrition and consistent guidance could help them feel better. In 2020, she started Dharani's Wellness Center — not as a business, but as a mission to help others live without pain and with real energy.
