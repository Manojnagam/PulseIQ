Founder's Story

A Mother's Transformation.
A Son's Mission.

"I watched my mother Lakshmidevi suffer for 18 years — fits, back pain, sciatica, knee pain, low BP, poor sleep — while raising our family. In 2020, the right nutrition changed everything for her. I was there for every step of that transformation. That's when I knew this was my path too."

From 2020, Bharath Nagam worked alongside his mother at Dharani's Wellness Center, Nandyal — helping customers, learning body composition analysis, nutrition coaching, and every detail of running a wellness center. He also coached clients online, reaching people beyond Nandyal.

On May 11th, 2026, Bharath brought that same mission to Kurnool — opening BK's Prime Wellness Studio. The same family values, the same proven approach, now available in person for the people of Kurnool.
